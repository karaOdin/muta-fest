Our Guests

Amina Bouayach

Morocco
Writer & Human Rights Activist

Amina Bouayach is a prominent Moroccan human rights activist and writer. She has dedicated her career to promoting human rights and social justice throughout the Mediterranean region. Her literary works explore themes of identity, migration, and cultural dialogue.

Marco Bellardi

Italy
Poet & Translator

Marco Bellardi is an Italian poet and translator known for his work bridging Mediterranean cultures through literature. His poetry collections have been translated into multiple languages, and he specializes in contemporary Arabic and Mediterranean poetry translation.

Leila Othmani

Tunisia
Filmmaker & Visual Artist

Leila Othmani is a Tunisian filmmaker and visual artist whose work focuses on Mediterranean stories and cross-cultural narratives. Her documentaries have been featured in international film festivals, exploring themes of identity and belonging.

Carlos Mendez

Spain
Musician & Composer

Carlos Mendez is a Spanish musician and composer who blends traditional Mediterranean sounds with contemporary music. His compositions celebrate the rich musical heritage of the Mediterranean basin and promote cultural dialogue through music.

Samir Kasemi

Albania
Author & Cultural Critic

Samir Kasemi is an Albanian author and cultural critic whose works examine the intersection of Balkan and Mediterranean cultures. His novels and essays have gained international recognition for their insightful portrayal of contemporary Mediterranean society.

Ivo Saglietti

Italy
Photographer & Journalist

Ivo Saglietti is an Italian photographer and journalist who has documented Mediterranean cultures for over two decades. His photographic exhibitions capture the essence of Mediterranean life and the connections between different coastal communities.
